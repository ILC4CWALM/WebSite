CWALM Goals
  • New theoretical approach to overcome the traditional description of Arabic linguistic system.
  • Final test model that aims to be the first large-scale validated CWA resource.

CWALM Corpus

We collected corpus data from various social media platforms to encompass a wide range of user-generated content. This corpus includes data from Moroccan dialect, ensuring comprehensive coverage of Colloquial Arabic Varieties.

DiMorph Tool

DiMorph: Dialect Morphological Analyzer is a morphological analyzer specifically tailored to Moroccan Arabic, contributing to automatic Part-of-Speech (POS) tagging and syntactic analysis.
